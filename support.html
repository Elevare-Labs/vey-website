Frequently asked questions

Quick answers.

How do I reset my password?

Open Vey, select the password reset option on the sign-in screen and enter the email address connected to your account. You will receive a secure password reset email.

I did not receive an email from Vey

Check your spam or junk folder and confirm that you entered the correct email address. You may need to wait briefly before requesting another email.

How do I delete my account?

Use the account deletion function inside the Vey app or submit a request through our account deletion page.

What information should I include in a bug report?

Include your device model, Android version, app version and a short description of the steps that caused the issue. Screenshots are helpful but should not contain passwords or other sensitive information.

Where can I learn how Vey handles personal data?

Details about the processing and protection of personal data will be available in our Privacy Policy.
